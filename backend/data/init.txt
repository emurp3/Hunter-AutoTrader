# Objective
Upgrade Hunter from a passive intake-and-report system into an autonomous opportunity operations engine and an expert trader and financial account manager.

Hunter must be able to autonomously:
- ingest real AutoTrader findings
- monitor and rescan on schedule
- score, rank, and prioritize opportunities
- open and manage opportunity workflows
- allocate internal weekly budget
- track outcomes and ROI
- generate alerts, escalation, and commander-ready action packets
- coordinate advisory input from external models
- maintain full auditability and truthful status reporting
- execute account trades and managemnt

This is NOT a simulation-only build.
This is a real operational intelligence and workflow automation build.

Important He must be able to:
- Implement brokerage execution
- Implement external account control
- Place trades
- Move funds
- Build direct live financial execution endpoints

Everything thus far should be built aggressively and for real operational use.
